The Future Of Alpha

Future of our Country

  • Supporting Brothers with organizing policy actions, protests, and voting drives - through Eastern Region Alpha Advocacy Arm
  • Assist with the coordination of voter information and registration events throughout the region for local, state and federal races
  • Continue to reimagine the work of our national programs as they strenghten our country


The Future of the House

  • Develop a way that alumni chapters are notified when a college brother graduate and relocates to the area so we can increase retention
  • Host Virtual bi-monthly open townhalls so Brothers can air concerns, get updates and provide feedback on proposed changes and actions
  • Work to brainstorm ways to diversity the Fraternity's revenues


The Future of Brothers Finances

  • Build a master class podcast and or blog where Brothers can discuss policy, launching businesses, home purchasing, grad school process and etc.
  • Work to build partnerships with recruiters, grad schools and organizations so Brothers can secure jobs and opportunities
  • Work to build a database of Brothers in the Eastern Region willing to mentor and assist other Brothers
  • Strenthen our C2C program and emphasizing a board room component so Brothers can serve on organizations board of directors
  • Support Brothers on launching businesses through organizing think tanks and social media campaigns


The Future of Brothers Health

  • Expand resources, conversations and events concerning Brothers physical & mental health
  • Organizing courageous conversations converning Title IX and consent and work with the Brotherhood to incoporate it into IMDP
  • Facilitate spaces to discuss being Black men in America


The Future of Brothers Health

  • Set up a small group of advisors/consultants that chapters can call on if they are in need of support fundraising, retention, internal mangement, and etc
  • Develop a fund that chapters can utilize for chapter affairs and programming if needed due to COVID-19 programming restrictions
  • Set-up a pandemic preparedness plan for the future of Alpha by finding virtual means of conducting business to help with creating a more efficient network for chapters and finances
